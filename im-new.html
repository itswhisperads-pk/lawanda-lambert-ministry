FAQ

When coming to Transformation Church for the first time, you may have some questions...

We have street parking available all around the church building. There are also several parking lots within a short walking distance. We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes early to find a spot.

Come as you are! You'll see everything from jeans and t-shirts to suits and ties. We care more about you than what you're wearing.

We have a fantastic Kids Ministry for children from birth through grade 5. Your kids will be cared for in a safe, fun, and age-appropriate environment while you enjoy the service.

Yes! We have a ramp entrance and an elevator to access all levels of the building. Our team is ready to assist you with any specific needs.

Coffee is served in our lobby before and after the service. Feel free to grab a cup and bring it into the sanctuary with you.

Our services typically last about 90 minutes. We begin with passionate worship music, followed by a biblical message that is relevant to your life.

The best way to get connected is to fill out a Connect Card or stop by the Welcome Center in the lobby. We'd love to meet you and help you find your next step.

Feel at home

No matter who you are or your background, you'll be welcome here. We hope that you feel at home, feel welcome, but most of all that you experience Jesus in a real way. We can't wait to meet you on Sunday!
