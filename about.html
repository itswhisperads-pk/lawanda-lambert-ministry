ABOUT

Beliefs, Values, Vision & Team

Our Mission

Transformation Church exists to make passionate disciples of Jesus that will have a significant and lasting impact on the city of Thunder Bay and beyond.

We believe in the power of community and the transformative love of Christ. Our doors are open to everyone, regardless of where they are in their spiritual journey.

OUR BELIEFS

  • We believe in one God, who is Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe the Bible is the inspired and holy Word of God.
  • We believe that we are saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ.
  • We believe in the sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in the second coming of Christ.

OUR VALUES

We believe the Bible is the final authority for our lives and ministry. It guides our decisions, shapes our culture, and defines our message.

We express our love for God through spirited and heartfelt worship. We believe worship is a lifestyle, not just a Sunday event.

We are created for relationship. We strive to build a community where people are known, loved, and cared for in genuine ways.

We are committed to helping people grow in their faith. We provide pathways for spiritual maturity and leadership development.

We exist for those who are not yet here. We are dedicated to serving our city and sharing the hope of Jesus with our neighbors.

VISION

Our vision is to be a multi-generational, multi-ethnic church that reflects the diversity of our city and the Kingdom of God.

We believe in multiplication. Our vision includes planting new life-giving churches in Thunder Bay and across Northern Ontario.

We want to see our city transformed by the love of God. We partner with local organizations to meet physical and spiritual needs.
